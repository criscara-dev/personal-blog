# About

Hi! I’m Cristian Caratti and this is my personal blog: a single home for long-form writing mostly about tech, physical practice, and art.

I build things with software and solve problems with people. Almost ten years in tech — web development, support engineering, technical account management across financial services, healthcare, and analytics SaaS. BCS-certified.

The common thread across all of it: I like going deep into how things work, then making that complexity useful for someone else. Whether that's building something from scratch, automating a workflow for an internal team, or helping a client actually get value out of the software they're paying for — that's where the work feels right. Client enabler. Problem solver. Builder.

I write here about tech, what I'm building, and how I think about things. I also write about my other practices — movement and art — because I keep finding the same ideas turning up across all three. There's more overlap between physical practice, making things, and building software than most people assume.
